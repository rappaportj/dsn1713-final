Our Story

In 1976, Book City opened its doors to the people of Toronto. Situated in the Annex, one of Toronto's great neighbourhoods, it's been treating its customers to an eclectic range of books ever since. Toronto bibliophiles must have liked us — because forty years on, we now have four locations across the city.

Book City was founded and is still operated by a family that goes back four generations in the book business. Perhaps this is why we stick so closely to a more traditional method of bookselling. We jam our bookstores full of new and backlist titles, pack our tables high with bargains, and staff our stores with people who love reading as much as our customers do.

We believe in the power of books to change lives, and we are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive space for all readers. Whether you're a lifelong book lover or just starting your reading journey, we invite you to explore our stores and discover the magic of books.
