The Enchanted Forest

By Lena Hartwood

$89.74

Step into a world where magic breathes through the trees and secrets whisper in the wind.

When sixteen-year-old Elara stumbles upon a hidden path in the woods behind her grandmother's cottage, she enters the Enchanted Forest — a place of breathtaking beauty and ancient mystery./p>

But the forest is more than it seems. Shadows move between the trees, time bends and folds, and creatures of legend walk under the moonlight.

As Elara uncovers her connection to this mystical realm, she learns that the balance between light and darkness is crumbling. An ancient curse is awakening, and only she holds the key to restoring harmony — if she can survive the trials that await.

Blending lush world-building with heart‑pounding adventure, The Enchanted Forest is a spellbinding tale of courage, destiny, and the power of believing in what lies beyond the ordinary.

Perfect for fans of The Night Circus and The Starless Sea, Lena Hartwood's mesmerizing debut will transport readers into a world they'll never want to leave.
